My Life

My name is Uri Darom. I was born in Tel-Aviv, Israel, and moved to the United States when I was 8 years old. Currently, I am a senior at the Cambridge Rindge and Latin high school. I hope to major in computer science in college.

In my free time, I enjoy reading, photography, and astronomy. I do astrophotography at night, to which I have dedicated the majority of this site. Check out some of my images in the "Images" tab above.
